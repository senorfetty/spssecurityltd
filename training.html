Recruitement

Guard Recruiting Guidelines

SPS traditional recruiting procedures, which attract quality applicants from diverse fields, through colleges, employee referrals, seminars, newspaper advertising, brochures, and trade shows. Each employee or trainee must undergo criminal background check, fingerprinting, and SPS Security Services pre-employment screening before being hired with SPS and Investigations. Each employee must participate in an annual training which involves state laws, patrol techniques, and self-defense. SPS Services manager is personally in charge of verifying that each guard is neatly kitted. SPS Services special safety certifications courses before being authorized to carry work.


Requirements.

  • Must be a Kenyan Citizen.
  • Age 18-45 years.
  • A letter from previous employer if previously employed.

Training

SPS has implemented a three-fold training program that is hard to beat.

  • Drills training manual developed by qualified security officers
  • A self-paced, customer care training program developed by marketing department
  • A peer/supervisor facilitated field training program for all new security officers

The subject areas covered are:

  • Technical security measures
  • Foot drills
  • Terrorism and criminality observation techniques
  • Company code of conduct
  • VHF radio communication
  • Search procedures
  • Emergencies plan
  • Power of arrest and level of force
  • Report writing and record keeping
